# GentleCare Dental Clinic Chatbot - Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## 1. Project Overview

The GentleCare Dental Clinic AI Chatbot is designed to assist patients with appointment booking, service queries, clinic timings, pricing inquiries, and emergency guidance. It provides 24/7 automated support and aims to reduce manual workload at the clinic while improving patient experience.

## 2. Objectives

* Automate common patient queries.
* Reduce receptionist workload by at least 40%.
* Improve appointment scheduling efficiency.
* Provide quick and consistent responses.
* Offer a modern, AI-enabled support experience.

## 3. Scope

### In Scope

* Appointment booking flow.
* Consultation and service FAQs.
* Clinic hours and location details.
* Pricing information.
* Emergency guidance message.
* Contact and callback request.

### Out of Scope

* Real-time doctor assignment.
* Payments inside the chatbot.
* Medical diagnosis.

## 4. Key Features

* **Interactive appointment scheduling**
* **Service information** (cleaning, root canal, braces, whitening, etc.)
* **Automated FAQ responses**
* **Clinic timing + map location**
* **Emergency quick-action message**
* **Human handover option**

## 5. User Personas

### Patient Persona

* Age 16–60
* Needs quick answers about dental services
* Wants to book an appointment with minimal waiting

### Clinic Staff

* Receptionists reviewing chatbot appointments
* Dentists who receive patient details

## 6. Requirements

### Functional Requirements

1. The chatbot must allow booking appointments for available dates and times.
2. The chatbot must provide information about all dental services.
3. The chatbot must give clinic hours and address.
4. The chatbot must route emergency queries to a predefined message.
5. The chatbot must offer an option to talk to a human.

### Non-Functional Requirements

1. The system should respond within 2–3 seconds.
2. Should be compatible with web and mobile browsers.
3. Easy to maintain and update.

## 7. Tech Stack

* **Voiceflow** (chatbot build)
* **GitHub Pages** (hosting demo site)
* **HTML/CSS** (embedded prototype and demo video)

## 8. Success Metrics

* At least 70% of common queries handled automatically.
* Appointment booking drop-off reduced by 30%.
* User satisfaction score above 4.5/5.

## 9. Assumptions

* Clinic staff will update available time slots weekly.
* User has internet access.

## 10. Risks

* Voiceflow link expiry (backup demo video included).
* Incorrect user input may cause booking errors.

## 11. Deliverables

* Voiceflow AI chatbot prototype.
* Embedded demo HTML page.
* GitHub repository containing project files.
* Project documentation (BRD, Flow Diagram, Screens).
